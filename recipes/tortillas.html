Tortillas

Description

This is the recipe for a dish me and my girlfriend simply love. We call it tortillas but it's techically just chicken fajitas. It's a wonderful dish filled with lots of vegetables and chicken, so it's really healthy. We love to make ours a bit spicy, but not everyone does. If you do like spicier meals we suggest adding some chilli paprikas. You can heat up the tortillas in a pan and then each person fills their own, or you can fill the tortillas beforehand and put them in the oven for 5min. Read more to find out how we make our fajitas.

Ingredients

What we'll need for todays dish:

  • Tortillas (we use store bought ones, but if you know how to make your own even better)
  • Bell peppers (we use 3 colors so it looks better)
  • Brown beans
  • Chicken (you can also use beef)
  • Mushrooms
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Spices (we use red paprika, vegetables and chilli paprika)
  • Cheese (we mostly use cheddar)

Steps

How do you prepare fajitas?
  1. Chop up onions and put them in a pan
  2. After 3-4 minutes, when the onion is nicely done, add in the chicken
  3. After another 4-5 minutes add in the carrots which have been cut into small circles or strips
  4. After a few more minutes, add in the bell peppers
  5. after 5-10min add in the mushrooms and the beans
  6. Add all the spices you want like red paprika and Vegeta
  7. After the filling is done, heat up the tortillas on the stove.
  8. Just put the filling inside a tortilla, add in some shredded cheese, wrap it up and done
