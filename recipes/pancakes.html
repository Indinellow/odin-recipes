Pancakes

Description

This is a recipe i found on Betty Crocker and it looked so good i had to share it here. Pancakes are quick to make and above all delicious. The expeected prep time for pancakes is only 15min and it's never a bad tame for them. Let's see how to make great pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of whole wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 3 tablespoons baking powder
    • <1i>1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oul or melted butter

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, beat egg with wire whisk untill fluffy
  2. Stir in remaining ingredients until flour is moistened
  3. heat griddle or skillet over medium-high heat
  4. For each pancake, pour slightly less than 1/4 cup batter onto griddle
  5. cook 2 to 3 minutes or until bubbly on top and dry around edges
  6. Trun; cook other side until golden brown
